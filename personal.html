PERSONAL LOAN

A personal loan is an unsecured credit loan that does not require any collateral or security. This type of loan gives you the opportunity to borrow money without putting any of your assets at risk. The amount borrowed on personal loans can be used for a variety of purposes like consolidating debts, clearing unforeseen expenses, organizing a marriage, paying for a vacation, settling medical emergencies, etc. Personal loans typically have lower bank loan interest rates than credit cards, making them an attractive option for those who are looking to save money on interest payments. The sum acquired is repaid over time with interest and is sanctioned based on the repayment capabilities and creditworthiness of the applicant. It is a relatively quick and high funding process if met with the necessary prerequisites of the lender.

Types

Instant Loan:Instant personal loans have a very minimal and easy documentation process where the applicant can be sanctioned a loan within a few minutes to none.

Balance Transfer: The personal loan acquired from a lender can be transferred to a different lender if the user chooses to get a better deal or lower bank loan interest rates elsewhere.

Top-up Loan: The top-up is obtained on the existing loan if the consumer may require an additional amount to meet their financial requirements. Top-up loans are flexible and attractive when the customer has a clean record of his repayment history and has a satisfactory completion of specified EMIs with the lender.

Consumer Durable: Durable items such as furniture, capital equipment, tools, appliances, smartphones, tableware, etc can be obtained with a durable consumer loan over a specified period with or without down payments.

Short-Term: Short-term loans are loans extending over a maximum period of 12 months or less.
