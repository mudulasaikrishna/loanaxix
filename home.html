Types

Conventional Home Loan: Conventional home loan or home purchase loan is a type of loan issued for the purchase of a new or pre-owned home at interest rates as low as 8.05% with up to 90% of the total amount offered by many lenders.

Land Purchase Loan: If you are looking forward to saving funds for later and investing in land before constructing your dream home, several banks offer a wide range of deals with or without down payments in a 100% paperless manner at interest rates as low as 8.1%.

Home Construction Loan: While purchasing land can be a burden itself, constructing a home is one step closer to making your dream come true. With home construction loans, owners or co-owners can receive funds on the total construction cost of the residential property.

Home Expansion Loan: A home extension or expansion loan is applied by individuals seeking to add an extra space or floor to the existing model. If you are planning to alter the current structure of your house, check out the enticing offers from India’s top financial lenders at LoanAxix with minimal documentation procedure and interest rates starting from 6.90% onwards.

Home Improvement Loan: Renovation adds a new look to the home during a big event. Even if there is no event to celebrate, attending to repairs and keeping the interiors and exteriors squeaky clean will make your house look better. What’s more attractive than top financiers offering funds at lower interest rates and zero processing fees?

Home Conversion Loan: Borrowers who have already taken out a home loan but wish to sell their property and purchase a new one can opt for a home conversion loan. This type of loan allows borrowers to purchase a new residential property by transferring their existing loan to the new house. Although this might be the most expensive option out there, you have nothing to be worried about at LoanAxix.

Bridge Loan: A bridge loan is a short-term financing option that can be used by homeowners to finance the purchase of a new home by selling their existing one. The loan amount can be used to cover the costs of the new home until the old property is sold. Moreover, at LoanAxix, we work with our partners to bring you the best financing experience for a bridge loan.

NRI Home Loan: For non-resident Indians planning to purchase a residential property in India, NRI home loans offer several benefit schemes to encourage our international residents to secure financial transactions.

Balance Transfer Loan: At any given time if you may wish to transfer your outstanding loan amount to a different lender with added benefits and lower interest rates, you may do so with any of the banks offering largely attractive and revised services.

PMAY: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana or PMAY is a beneficial subsidized loaning scheme registered only under a few banks that are allowed to provide funding with CLSS under PMAY for first-time property owners to avail of the benefits.

Eligibility Criteria

Age: 18 – 65 years

Type of Employment: Salaried/Self-Employed/Business Owner(Govt, Large Enterprises, Etc.)

Nationality: Resident of India/NRI

Income: Monthly Minimum ~ Rs. 20,000/- and above (Salaried/Self-Employed), Minimum ~ Rs. 2,00,000/- p.a onwards (Business Owners)

Maximum EMI: EMI ~ maximum of 50% – 70% of the net monthly income

Credit/CIBIL Score: Any profile(Credit score ~ 650 or above has a higher chance of getting a quick and instant loan with high funding at lower interest rates)

Employment Stability: 1 – 5 years and above (With a stable income and continual flow of money)
